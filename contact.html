How I made this "Website"

The core contents of the page is made in HTML files. These files decided what shows up on the page. The information is laid out from top to bottom within tags that determine whether something is plain text, title, an interactive element of the page, etc. Then there's the CSS file. This decides the styling of the webpages. (What fonts text appear as, colors, sizing, etc.) This is just a simple website layout that was drilled into my head in highschool from a webdesign class.
