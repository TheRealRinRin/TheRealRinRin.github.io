  • “Kanye West – I Thought about Killing You Lyrics | Genius Lyrics.” Genius, 1 June 2018,

    https://genius.com/Kanye-west-i-thought-about-killing-you-lyrics 
    This website has the official lyrics to Kanye West’s song I Thought about Killing You, from his album, Ye. This song is a two way conversation with himself and is a dive into how he feels about his bipolar disorder.
It also provides an artistic view into how he uses music to express changes in his life.

  • Mayo Clinic Staff. “Bipolar Disorder — Symptoms & Causes.” Mayo Clinic, 14 Aug. 2024,

    Mayo Clinic is a nonprofit, integrated (hospital + research + education + institution) academic medical center.
It is one of the largest and most respected medical organizations, known globally for high-quality patient care, research, and medical education.

  • Monk-Payton, Brandy. “The Art of Notoriety in Kanye West’s Persona.” Persona Studies, Deakin University, 17 June 2023,

    https://ojs.deakin.edu.au/index.php/ps/article/view/1614 
    This article analyzes Kanye West’s public image as a carefully constructed persona that blends artistry, controversy, and vulnerability.
Monk-Payton argues that Kanye’s notoriety is not accidental but part of his ongoing negotiation between authenticity and spectacle.
The piece also explores how the media and audience participation contribute to his larger cultural narrative.

  • Winton, Richard. “Authorities Spent 2 Hours Persuading Kanye West to Be Hospitalized, Sources Say.” Los Angeles Times, 23 Nov. 2016,
